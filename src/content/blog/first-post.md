## Welcome to the Accletra Blog!

This is where we'll share news, updates, and thoughts about web development, SaaS, and the Astro framework.

### Why We Chose Astro

Astro is a modern static site builder that delivers lightning-fast performance with a next-gen developer experience. We chose it for Accletra because of its focus on content-rich websites and its innovative "islands architecture."

We're excited to be on this journey and look forward to sharing more with you soon!
